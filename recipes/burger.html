High Protein Chicken Burger

A lovely brioche bun topped with kewpie mayo, lettuce and a juicy chicken patty! This burger is low in calories and high in protein! The perfect meal for an active lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 4 Brioche buns
  • 500g Chicken Mince
  • 40g Crumbs - Kellogg's
  • 60g Iceberg Lettuce
  • 150g Kewpie Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tsp Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tsp Paprika

Steps

  1. Start by adding 500g of chicken mince to a bowl with 1 Tsp of paprika, 1 Tsp of onion powder, 1 Tsp of garlic powder, 2 Tsp of salt & 2 Tsp of pepper. Mix well with hands.
  2. Add baking paper to tray and lightly spray with cooking oil. Form chicken into 4 patties on tray and place in freezer for 30 minutes.
  3. Put 40g of crumbs onto plate with a dash of the seasonings above.
  4. Take patties out of freezer and evenly coat with crumbs, then spray with cooking oil.
  5. Air fry at 200C for 20 minutes/Oven bake at 200C for 20 minutes, flipping at half way.
  6. Toast the brioche buns on a pan with the stove on medium heat.
  7. Add one chicken patty to each bottom bun followed by a handful of shredded lettuce, then spread 37g of kewpie mayo on each top bun.
  8. Enjoy! extra: Add a slice of cheese to elevate taste!
